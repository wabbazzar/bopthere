## Bingo Rules:
- Each participant gets the same 25 prompts arranged randomly on their 5x5 board
- Take a photo when you witness each moment to "claim" that square
- Photos will be shared Sunday evening for everyone to see
- First to get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!
- Bonus: Most creative photo interpretation gets a special prize

*Remember: The goal is to create memories together, not compete too seriously. Have fun and be respectful when capturing moments!*
